First and foremost, you are encountering this error because Maven cannot locate the dependency in your local repository. Moreover, this is often caused by a stale cache or a missing entry in your configuration.

To begin, you will want to open your pom.xml file and verify that the dependency has been added correctly. Additionally, it is important to note that you should always double-check the group ID and the artifact ID. After that, the next step is to force Maven to refresh its dependencies, which can be accomplished by running a clean install. Finally, you may also want to delete your local repository folder to ensure a truly clean slate.

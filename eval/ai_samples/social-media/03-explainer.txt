Great question! Slow home Wi-Fi is a common frustration, and I'm happy to help you understand the root causes. Let's dive into the key factors.

First, router placement is crucial. A centrally located router ensures robust, seamless coverage throughout your home. Second, interference from other devices can significantly degrade performance. Third, outdated hardware may bottleneck your connection.

Here are the key takeaways to optimize your network:
- Position your router centrally and elevated.
- Leverage the 5GHz band for high-speed devices.
- Upgrade to a modern, future-proof router or mesh system.

By implementing these strategies, you can dramatically enhance your connectivity and enjoy a smoother online experience. Ultimately, a well-optimized network empowers seamless streaming, gaming, and productivity. Hope this helps, and happy browsing!

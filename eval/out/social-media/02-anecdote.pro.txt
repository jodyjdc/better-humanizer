So the airline lost my bag last week and I'm honestly still a little mad about it. It was a connecting flight and the bag just didn't make it onto the second plane. Then nobody could tell me where it actually was for three whole days, which is the part that really got me.

The first night I ended up in a hotel with basically nothing on me. No toothbrush, no clean shirt, not even a phone charger that fit. So there I am at some pharmacy at eleven at night buying the saddest little travel kit you have ever seen in your life.

And the airline was no help at all. They kept saying they would call me back and then they never once called me back. So I called them. Every single day. And every single day it was the exact same script from a different person who clearly could not do anything.

Anyway the bag turned up eventually. Turns out it had been sitting at the wrong airport the entire time and nobody thought to mention that. So yeah, pack a change of clothes in your carry-on. That is the one thing I would actually tell people from all of this.
